PLATFORM PLAYBOOK — Foundational Rules
📸 INSTAGRAM
Best formats: Reels (9:16) for reach · Carousels (4:5) for saves & permanence · Single posts only for hero shots/announcements
Image specs: 4:5 vertical (1080x1350) for max feed real estate · Square 1080x1080 for graphics
Video specs: 9:16, 1080x1920, 15–60s · Use trending audio from Reels Audio tab
Caption length: 100–200 words ideal · Line breaks every 1–2 sentences for mobile readability
Hashtags: 8–15 in first comment (not main caption) · Mix big + niche tags
Pin strategy: Pin highest-performing Reel weekly · Pin 3 grid posts representing brand pillars
🎵 TIKTOK
Best formats: Native vertical video ONLY · 21–34s sweet spot · 60s for emotional/storytelling
Hook rule: First 1.5 seconds must stop the scroll — loudest visual, biggest text, boldest line
Subtitles required: 60% of TikTok is watched on mute — burn captions in
Audio: Use TRENDING sounds from Discover page · Refresh weekly · Trending sound > great audio
Hashtags: 4–6 in caption · Mix #fyp + niche + brand · Keep caption under 150 chars
NEVER: Cross-post videos with TikTok watermark · Static graphics · Long intros
👤 FACEBOOK
Best formats: Photo carousels (3–7 images) outperform video · Single hero photo for cocktails · Events for tournaments
Image specs: Square 1080x1080 or landscape 1200x630 · 4:5 also works
Caption length: 150–400 words · FB users actually read · Always include address + phone + hours
Engagement: Tag prompts ('Tag a friend who...') drive top-3 engagement format · Comments boost reach more than likes
Local tools: Always create Facebook Events for milestones · Boost posts $30–100 for special nights · Geo-target Clifton/Passaic
Tag visible people: When customers appear in photos, tag them — they share to their own profile = free reach
